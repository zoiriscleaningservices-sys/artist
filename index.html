From The Streets To The Studio

Luciano 4E (born December 13, 1999) is a Cuban-American Latin urban artist, rapper, singer, and songwriter. Known for his fusion of reggaeton, Latin trap, rap, and other Latin music styles, he brings the energy and rhythms of Latin culture to the global music scene.

Luciano 4E began his musical journey as a teenager, experimenting with songwriting, recording, and performing.
